Mythos Books

Excellence in Science Fiction and Fantasy

New York University Summer Publishing Institute is the parent company of Mythos Books. Along with Mythos Books, NYU-SPI has a number of other imprints. Dedicated to excellence in publishing for 37 years.

Imprint 1 is NYU's Children's Publishing Group.

Imprint 2 is NYU's Entertainment Group

Imprint 3 is NYU's Graphic Novel Group

Imprint 4 is NYU's Travel Group

Imprint 5 is NYU's Mystery publishing group.

Imprint 6 is NYU's Nonfiction publishing group.

Imprint 7 is NYU's Romance publishing group.

At Mythos Books, we focus on bringing cultural diversity to the science fiction and fantasy realm. Mythos draws from both literary fiction and genre fiction, past and future in order to create new narratives now. We believe that the science fiction and fantasy authors we champion bring light to our own culturally and ethnically diverse world. Our writers pull from their unique backgrounds to bring compelling, beautiful stories and worlds to our readers.

Imprint 9 is Self-Help publishing group.

Imprint 10 is NYU's Young Adult publishing group.
