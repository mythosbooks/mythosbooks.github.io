Mythos Books

Excellence in Science Fiction and Fantasy

New York University Summer Publishing Institute is the parent company of Mythos Books. Along with Mythos Books, NYU-SPI has a number of other imprints. NYU-SPI has just rebranded their 10 imprints for the Summer of 2015. NYU-SPI Group: dedicated to excellence in publishing for 37 years.

Imprint 1 is NYU's Children's Publishing Group.

Showcase Books is NYU's Entertainment Group

Imprint 3 is NYU's Graphic Novel Group

Hummingbird Press provides new perspectives on exploring culture through travel and lifestyle. We encourage our readers to discover the world by incorporating tips and guides into our creative narratives. From memoirs and coffee table books to interactive ebooks, we have a wide range of ways through which our readers can experience the journeys of their favorite characters, people and stories.

Imprint 5 is NYU's Mystery publishing group.

At Candor Press, we publish with a cause. We tell nonfiction narratives about human experiences and the people who stand up for them. Our titles provide a fresh perspective on poignant social issues told through the stories of the individuals they affect most. We aim to amplify the voices of young thought leaders navigating the changing tides of human rights as they share diverse takes on the universal experiences of being human.

Magnetic Books is an imprint for familiar readers who know and love the romance genre, but want something different. Championing the concept of female empowerment, our titles revolve around strong female leads that all of our readers can relate to. While we aim to give our readers a high-octane, visceral experience in all of our romantic suspense titles, we also make sure to deliver the consuming intensity and electrifying passion that our readers associate with the romance genre. We keep it thrilling. We keep it sexy. We keep it Magnetic.

Mythos Books is NYU's refurbished Science Fiction and Fantasy imprint. At Mythos Books, we focus on bringing cultural diversity to the science fiction and fantasy realm, simultaneously drawing from both literary fiction and genre fiction. Established 2015.

Imprint 9 is Self-Help publishing group.

Imprint 10 is NYU's Young Adult publishing group.
