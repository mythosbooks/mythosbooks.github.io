Daina Chaviano, born in Havana Cuba, was a literary star at a young age. In 1979 she received the David Prize for Science Fiction. Since then she has solidified her clout in the Science Fiction genre. Daina established the first science fiction literary workshop in Latin America and later worked as a screenwriter for television shows for children, youths, and adults. She was a TV and radio emcee, and acted in some independent films. Daina has been living in Miami, Florida since 1991. This SEASON, Daina will be publishing BLANK with Mythos Books. To see a sneak peek, and learn more about Daina's other works, check out her website!

Zen Cho was born and raised in Malaysia and currently lives in England. She mostly writes speculative fiction, with the occasional foray into romance. Her ideal book would be Anne of Green Gables, but set in Asia. She has recently published a short story collection, called Spirits Abroad, and has recently edited a Malaysian cyberpunk anthology called Cyberpunk: Malaysia, which came out in summer 2015. Her forthcoming novel, TITLE, will debut with Mythos Books this SEASON. Check out her website for sneak peeks of the upcoming novel!
