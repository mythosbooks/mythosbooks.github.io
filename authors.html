Mythos Authors

Excellence in Science Fiction and Fantasy

Margaret Peterson Haddix is an author reknown for her books.

Daina Chaviano, born in Havana Cuba, was a literary star at a young age. In 1979 she received the David Prize for Science Fiction. Since then she has solidified her clout in the Science Fiction genre.

Daina established the first science fiction literary workshop in Latin America and later worked as a screenwriter for television shows for children, youths, and adults. She was a TV and radio emcee, and acted in some independent films. Daina has been living in Miami, Florida since 1991.

This SEASON, Daina will be publishing BLANK with Mythos Books. To see a sneak peek, and learn more about Daina's other works, check out her website!

Zen Cho was born and raised in Malaysia and currently lives in England. She mostly writes speculative fiction, with the occasional foray into romance.

"My ideal book would probably be a version of Anne of Green Gables, set in Asia. With dragons!"says Zen. She has recently published a short story collection, called Spirits Abroad, and has recently edited a Malaysian cyberpunk anthology called Cyberpunk: Malaysia, which came out in summer 2015.

Her forthcoming novel, A Something Of Dreams, will debut with Mythos Books this season. Check out her website for sneak peeks of the upcoming novel!
