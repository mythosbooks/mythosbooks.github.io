Mythos Books

Excellence in Science Fiction and Fantasy

At Mythos Books,we focus on bringing cultural diversity to the science fiction and fantasy realm. Mythos draws from both literary fiction and genre fiction—past and future—to create new narratives now. We believe that the authors we champion bring light to this culturally and ethnically diverse world we all inhabit. Our writers pull from their unique personal histories to bring compelling stories and faraway worlds to curious readers.

Mythos is a new imprint of the NYU SPI Publishing Group. We are exciting to be bringing the stories of Margaret Peterson Haddix, Daina Chaviano, and Ken Liu to our readers. If you'd like to know a little more about who runs our enterprise, check out our staff page.
