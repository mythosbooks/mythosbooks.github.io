Here are our upcoming fantasy titles

Ken Liu’s The Festival of Dreams is a high fantasy novel brimming with decadence and deception, an engrossing tale unfolding over generations to a thrilling ending. A former peasant is thrilled in her new role as queen, excited to see the inner workings of the Kingdom she is now part of. During the annual festival between Kingdoms, done to appease the old Gods, she makes a shocking discovery that sets her on a path towards vengeance- against her enemies, her family, and the gods themselves.

The Festival of Dreams will be released February 7, 2017.

England, 1862. Darwin’s theories have rocked the world, no more so than at Oxford University, where a group of ambitious young naturalists are forced to reevaluate everything they thought was true. In the pursuit of knowledge, the group sets sail down the Amazon River to create a document heretofore thought impossible: a comprehensive list of all creatures in the area. It is there that they make a shocking discovery, a cover of mermaids who defy the natural world and the ideals of science all in one. If the two groups are two survive, they have to learn to trust each other.

The Iaras Upstream will be released June 6, 2017
